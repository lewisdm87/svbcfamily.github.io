Shenandoah Valley Baptist Church

Presents

The Talleys

Come out and join us Sunday June 02 at 6pm for a family-friendly concert provided by The Talleys. There will not be any charge, but a free-will offering will received for the Talleys to support their ministry.


The Talleys are a mainstay of Christian music, having been Awarded a Dove Award and numerous Singing News Fan Awards. Roger, Debra (wife), and Lauren (daughter) have been performing together since 1996.


You can find more information about The Talleys on their website here or sign-up to attend the event on the SVBC Facebook Page.
