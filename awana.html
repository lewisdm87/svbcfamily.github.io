Awana Clubs

Our Mission

Awana's goal at Shenandoah Valley Baptist Church is to reach boys and girls with the gospel of Christ and train them to serve Him. We have four groups in our club... Cubbies, Spars, Truth & Training, and Trek.

In Awana we have quarterly themes, exciting songs, Bible stories, interactive games, and small group time learning verses.

Age Ranges

Cubbies : Pre-School
Sparks : K5 - 2nd Grade
Truth & Training : 3rd - 5th Grade
Trek : 6th - 8th Grade

Meetings

Awana Clubs meets ever Wednesday night September - May from 6:30pm - 8:00pm at the Chruch.

We meet in teh gym and end our night downstairs in the main building.
