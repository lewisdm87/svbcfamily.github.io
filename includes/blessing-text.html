10 Ways to Be a Blessing During the Virus Issue

  1. Elderly - Be sure to check on them often, be willing to shop or go to the pharmacy if needed.

  2. Neighbors - Drop off a note card letting them know that you are praying for them during this time. Be sure to include your contact info in the note.

  3. Service Personnel - Notes and cards of encouragement, drop off some baked goods by the various stations.

  4. Single moms - Contact ABBA Care (540-665-9660) (www.abbacare.org) to see what supplies or ministry is needed. Order a pizza and have it sent to them, drop off a family focused/fun DVD and some popcorn.

  5. Small business - Support, if you use the drive-thru leave an encouraging card or note. If you need to order something online, consider the “little guy” first.

  6. Medical Pro’s - Text or email words of prayer and encouragement thanking them for their care and concern for the community.

  7. Schools - Offer to help pack meals or provide needed items (gift cards are always welcomed).

  8. Homeless - Contact the local mission(s) and be willing to volunteer time and/or resources.

  9. Red Cross - Donate blood or offer our facilities for a blood drive.

  10. Pray, Pray, Pray!!!
